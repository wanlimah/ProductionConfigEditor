📋 WHAT IS IT?
   Add multiple product names to your configuration at once!
   Perfect for when you need to configure 10, 20, or even 100 products.

🚀 HOW TO USE IT (3 SIMPLE STEPS)
   
   Step 1: Navigate to Step 2 in the wizard
   └─ Select your configuration from the dropdown
   
   Step 2: Click "📋 Bulk Add Products" button
   └─ It's right next to the "➕ Add Single Product" button
   
   Step 3: Enter product names and add!
   └─ Type or paste product names (one per line)
   └─ Review the preview on the right
   └─ Click "Add All Products"

📝 EXAMPLE INPUT

   AFEM-8266-AP1-RF1-QA
   AFEM-8267-AP1-RF1-QA
   WW-PROD
   WW-QA
   RS-PROD
   RS-QA

   ↓ Results in 6 packages added instantly!

✨ KEY FEATURES

   ✓ Auto-converts to UPPERCASE
   ✓ Removes empty lines automatically
   ✓ Detects and skips duplicates
   ✓ Shows live preview before adding
   ✓ Uses same attributes as existing packages

💡 PRO TIPS

   • Copy product names from Excel and paste directly
   • The attributes will match your existing packages
   • You can still edit individual packages after bulk adding
   • Don't forget to save your XML when done!

⚠️ REMEMBER

   Changes are NOT auto-saved!
   Always click "💾 Save" or "💾 Save As" after bulk adding.
