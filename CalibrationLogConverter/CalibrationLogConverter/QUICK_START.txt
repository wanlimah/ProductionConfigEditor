🔍 HOW THE PARSER WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The parser automatically finds columns by searching for these keywords:

MODEL:        model, equipment, instrument, device
SERIAL:       serial, serial number, s/n, sn  
DUE DATE:     due date, next cal, next calibration, expiry, cal due
CAL DATE:     cal date, calibration date, date calibrated, date
STATUS:       status, result, pass/fail
LOCATION:     location, site, facility
TECHNICIAN:   technician, tech, calibrated by, engineer
NOTES:        notes, remarks, comments

⚡ All searches are CASE-INSENSITIVE
⚡ Automatically SKIPS empty rows
⚡ Works with ANY sheet in the workbook
