Status Detection:
─────────────────
IF Model = "X" OR Serial Number = "X"
  THEN Status = "FAIL"
  ELSE Status = "PASS"

Due Date Display:
─────────────────
IF Due Date exists
  THEN Show formatted date (yyyy-MM-dd)
  ELSE Show "N/A"

Failed Records:
───────────────
BEFORE: Skipped (not in export)
AFTER:  Included with FAIL status
